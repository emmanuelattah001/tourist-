About the Event

The International Tourism Festival 2026 is more than a conference — it's a global celebration of travel, culture, and economic opportunity. Set against the breathtaking backdrop of Niagara Falls, this three-day festival brings together tourism professionals, business leaders, exhibitors, students, and travelers worldwide.

Expect a blend of world-class exhibitions, adventure-packed experiences, and high-value networking that highlight how tourism shapes economies, cultures, and communities.
